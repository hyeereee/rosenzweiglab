About us

We are a multidisciplinary lab led by Dr. Derek H. Rosenzweig at McGill University that studies musculoskeletal tissue modeling and engineering, predominantly using biofabrication approaches including 3D printing and bioprinting. We utilize these tools for tissue engineering applications but also cancer and tissue modeling.

Our research group is remarkably interdisciplinary. Our interests span biology, materials science, engineering and clinical applications. Visit people for more info!
